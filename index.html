Enumero Consulting

Who are we and what do we do?



Enumero Consulting is an independent consulting business. Our mission is to extract the knowledge from your data and generate meaningfull opportunities for you and your clients.



We have an expertise in analysing a wide range of datasets for stakeholders. We perform comprehensive data analyses for our clients and provide reports for various projects as needed.



Our expertive includes the quantitative and statistical analysis of a range of spatio-temporal, telemetry, sensors recordings and image datasets.



Our Products



Environmental Analysis

We produce a range of spatial and temporal statistical analyses tailored to any audience or purpose. We also produce technical documents, GIS mediums, and high-quality maps suitable for scientists and policy makers.

Telemetry Processing

Get the best out of your telemetry dataset. Following your specifications we process a wide range of telemetry datasets. We have experience with Argos-Linked Fastloc-GPS, Argos, acoustic tracking or light based geolocators technologies.

Sensor Analytics

We analyse a range of commonly used sensor readings. We have experience with processing meteorological, water quality, oceanic currents, accelerometer and remote sensing data using signal processing and statistical time series analyses.

Image Processing

We process images to extract all types of valuable information from them. We perform tailored analyses which includes detecting, counting or mesuring regions of interest on your images.
