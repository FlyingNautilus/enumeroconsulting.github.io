Who are we and what we do?



Enumero Consulting is an independent consulting business.



We have an expertise in analysing a wide range of datasets for stakeholders. We perform comphresenvice data analyses for our clients and provide reports for various projects as needed.



Our expertive includes the quantitative and statistical analysis of a range of spatio-temporal, telemetry, sensors recordings and image datasets.

Our Products



Environmental Analysis

We produce a range of spatial and temporal statistical analyses tailored to any audience or purpose. We also produce technical documents, GIS mediums, and high-quality maps suitable for scientists and policy makers.

Telemetry Data Analysis

Following your specifications Enumero Consulting process and clean a wide range of telemetry dataset. including: Argos-Linked Fastloc-GPS, Argos, acoustic tracking or light based geolocators.

Sensor Analytics

We analyse a range of commonly used sensor readings. We have experience with processing meteorological, water quality, oceanic currents, accelerometer and remote sensing data.

Image analysis

We process images to extract all types of valuable information from them. We perform tailored analyses which includes as detect, counting or mesuring regions of interest and we perfom comprehensive morphometric analyses.
